Our Story

How a love for pasta led to a gourmet food truck on the streets of Luxembourg.

From Passion to Pasta

It all began with a simple craving — real, handmade pasta that didn’t take hours to make or cost a fortune.

After years of traveling through Italy and working in restaurants across Europe, our founder returned to Luxembourg with a dream: bring the soul of Italian pasta-making to the streets — fast, fresh, and full of flavor.

And so, Pastalux was born — a food truck that serves only what we’d eat ourselves: pasta made daily, sauces simmered slowly, and ingredients sourced locally whenever possible.

We believe good food doesn’t need to be complicated — just made with care, served with a smile, and enjoyed wherever life takes you.
